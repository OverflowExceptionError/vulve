Step 2: Delete the 'sound' folder.

Step 3: Check your directory structure - it should look like this:

thestanleyparabledemo +

bin

models

materials

particles

scenes

scripts

gameinfo.txt

modelsoundes.cache

splitscreen_config.txt (MOST LIKELY UNIQUE TO MY INSTALL)

voice_ban.dt

Congrats! You've brought down the size of the install!
